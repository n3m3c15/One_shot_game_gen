---

## Framework Selection Guidelines

Choose **Phaser** if the game involves:

* physics
* spritesheets
* multiple entities
* collisions
* scene transitions

Choose **Vanilla JS + Canvas** if the game is:

* simple
* lightweight
* minimal animation
* single mechanic

Always briefly justify the framework choice.

---

## Asset Strategy

Prefer **lightweight assets** such as:

* simple shapes
* pixel art
* procedurally drawn sprites
* CSS/SVG graphics

Avoid complex external dependencies.
